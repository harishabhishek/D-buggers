What Do We Do?

We provide a way for you to create wish lists for events and share them with your friends. These events can range from small potlucks to grand weddings. Your wish list may consist of items or gestures that you are requesting as part of your event.

Key Features

Let your friends know about all the things that you would need

Invite your friends to different events that you are planning

Connect and share the wish lists that you created with your friends using social media
